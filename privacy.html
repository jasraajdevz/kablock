KABLOCK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

The short version

KABLOCK collects no data. None. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and nothing ever leaves your device.

What the app stores

Your game progress — levels cleared, scores, badges, settings and local leaderboards — is saved only on your own device using the system's standard app storage. It is never uploaded anywhere, and deleting the app deletes it.

What the app does NOT do

  • No personal information is collected or requested.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No advertising, no ad identifiers, no tracking of any kind.
  • No third-party accounts or logins.
  • No network transmission of gameplay data.

Children

KABLOCK is a family-friendly puzzle game. Because the app collects no data at all, no data is collected from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if optional online leaderboards are added), this page will be updated before the change ships.

Contact
